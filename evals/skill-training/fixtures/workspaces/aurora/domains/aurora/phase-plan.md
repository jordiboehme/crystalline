The phased delivery plan for Aurora, from first shipment through platform buildout. Three phases pace the program: what ships first, who receives it and the gate each phase must clear before the next begins.

Dates move with capacity; the gates and the order do not. Each phase closes with a written review before the next opens, re-baselined quarterly with the program board.

- [decision] The opening phase ships governed recovery to the five design partners before any platform work #plan
- [fact] The second phase begins only after the outcome ledger holds two full quarters of signals #plan
- [fact] The third phase lets agents propose reallocation moves under human approval #plan
