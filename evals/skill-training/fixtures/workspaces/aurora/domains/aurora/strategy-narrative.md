The strategic rationale for Aurora, the company's pivot from selling standalone dashboards to operating a governed insight platform. This narrative records why the program exists, who buys it, which capability ships first, how many connectors are in scope and what would make us stop.

Approved by the leadership team in the spring cycle, superseding the earlier concept memo. The bullets below are decisions, not aspirations.

- [decision] The purchase call sits with a three-seat committee: the operations chair, the finance owner and the technology validator #strategy
- [decision] The bet is walked back if fewer than three lighthouse customers renew by month eighteen #strategy
- [decision] The road not taken: bolt analytics onto our older suite and ride the installed base for another decade #strategy
- [decision] The first build focus is governed recovery with outcome-based planning, superseding the illustrative list in the platform overview #strategy
- [decision] Opening scope trims the connectors to four sources, not the dozen sketched in the platform overview #strategy
